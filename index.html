About the School

A Foundation Built on Faith & Learning

Islahiah School is a private Islamic school (Type 15/1) operating under the Office of the Private Education Commission (OPEC), Ministry of Education, Thailand. Originally founded as a pondok in 1946, formally registered in 1961, and continuously growing ever since.

We offer education from Kindergarten through Grade 12 as a full boarding school for both boys and girls, with strictly separated facilities in accordance with Islamic principles. Morning sessions are dedicated to Islamic studies; afternoons follow the Thai national curriculum.

The school occupies 32 rai in Sateng Nok, Mueang District, Yala — a campus designed for learning, community, and spiritual growth.
